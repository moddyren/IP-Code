Product Description

Standard:IEC 60529:1989 + A1:1999 + A2:2013 Clause 14.2.9, DIN40050-9, ISO20653 IP degrees of protection provided by enclosures for the electrical equipment in road vehicles for protecting the electrical equipment inside the enclosure against the effects of the ingress of solid foreign objects, including dust (protection against foreign objects), protecting the electrical equipment inside the enclosure against the effects of the ingress of water (protection against water).

The IPX9/IPX9K test chamber is designed for testing protection against high-pressure and high-temperature water jets. This chamber simulates conditions where enclosures might be subject to high-pressure, high-temperature cleaning or decontamination procedures.

The test chamber complies with IEC 60529 IPX9 and ISO 20653 IPX9K standards, providing comprehensive testing capabilities for automotive, marine, and industrial applications where equipment must withstand intense cleaning procedures.
