Product Description

The IP2X jointed test finger is designed to verify protection against access with a finger. This probe simulates a human finger to test whether a person is adequately protected against access to hazardous parts within equipment enclosures.

This test probe B conforms to the standard requirements of IEC61032 fig.2, IEC60529 IP2X and IEC60065. The jointed test finger features three articulated joints that can bend up to 90 degrees, allowing for comprehensive testing of potential access points.

Applications

  • Testing protection against finger access to hazardous parts
  • Verification of protection against objects greater than 12mm
  • IP2X protection rating certification testing
  • Quality control testing in manufacturing
  • Product safety compliance verification
