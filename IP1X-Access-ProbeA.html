Product Description

The IP1X test probe with 50mm diameter sphere is designed to verify protection against access with the back of hand. This probe is used to test whether a person is adequately protected against access to hazardous parts with the back of hand.

This test probe A conforms to the standard requirements of IEC61032 fig.1, IEC60529 IP1 and IEC60065, etc. This probe is intended to verify the protection of persons against access to hazardous parts. It is also used to verify the protection against access with the back of the hand.

Applications

  • Testing protection against access to hazardous parts with back of hand
  • Verification of protection against solid foreign objects ≥ 50 mm
  • IP1X protection rating certification testing
  • Quality control testing in manufacturing
  • Product safety compliance verification
